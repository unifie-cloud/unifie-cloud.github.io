Overview

Unifie streamlines application deployment to your own cloud account, empowering you with the flexibility and scalability you need to thrive.

Unifie help to DevOps engineers do a work faster and more than that - Unifie allow to DevOps move there work to non devops guys.

Effortless Deployment Streamlined​

One-click deployments​

Eliminate manual configuration with seamless Git integration, automated builds, and instant deployment capabilities.

Deploy anywhere​

Unifie adapts to your infrastructure, allowing deployment directly to your preferred cloud provider (AWS, GCP, Azure, and more) or your self-hosted Kubernetes cluster.

Unparalleled customization​

Take complete control over your Kubernetes environment. Configure everything to your specific needs and preferences, including network settings, resource allocation, and security policies.

Automatic scaling​

Dynamically scale your applications based on resource demands, ensuring optimal performance and cost efficiency. Tools like KEDA and VPA for seamless autoscaling are integrated by default.

Reduce costs​

Optimize your cloud spending with Unify's cost-saving features and avoid overpaying for resources you don't need.

Improve collaboration​

Streamline team workflows with user management, project collaboration tools, and a centralized platform for managing your cloud infrastructure.

Manage With Ease​

Resource allocation & Management​

Easily configure environment variables and networking for consistent deployment across environments, tailored to your app's needs.
